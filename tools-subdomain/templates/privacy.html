Privacy Policy Details

How the Calculator Tools Work

All calculator and formatting tools on this site run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. When you enter data into any tool — a number, a date, a block of text, or a JWT token — that data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to or stored on any BKX Labs server. Closing or refreshing the page clears all input immediately.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and pixel tags) to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your browsing history across sites. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

Cookie Consent (EEA and UK Visitors)

If you are visiting from the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you will be shown a cookie consent notice before any personalized ads are served. You may choose to allow or decline personalized advertising. If you decline, non-personalized ads may still be displayed. The consent management integration is handled through Google's certified consent management platform (CMP) and will be active once fully configured for this domain.

No Accounts, No Analytics Tracking of Personal Data

This site does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information directly. Any analytics data (such as aggregate page view counts) that may be collected by third-party services does not include the content you enter into any calculator tool.

Contact
